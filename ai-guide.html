💡 Kaam Ki Baatein
🗣️ Hindi ya English — dono chalenge. Jis bhaasha mein likho, AI usi mein jawab dega.
🔄 Pasand nahi aaya? "Wapas Jaao" dabao, kuch badlo, aur dobara generate karo.

🍽️ Recipe
Tum ek experienced Rajasthani chef ho jo vegetarian khana jaanta ho. Mujhe dal baati churma ki recipe chahiye 4 logon ke liye, lunch ke liye. Step-by-step Hindi mein batao, ingredients ki list ke saath. Pyaaz aur lahsun mat daalna.
✈️ Travel
Tum ek experienced travel guide ho jo India ke tours jaanta ho. Mujhe Goa ke liye 5 din ka trip plan chahiye — 2 adults aur 1 child (8 saal), December mein. Day-by-day format mein, medium budget mein. Sirf vegetarian restaurants suggest karo, bahut expensive jagah avoid karo.
✉️ Letter
Tum ek professional writer ho jo formal letters likhna jaanta ho. Mujhe Principal ke naam ek leave application likhni hai — 3 din ki medical leave ke liye. Formal English mein likho, lekin language simple rakho. Bahut complicated words mat use karo.
📚 Seekhna
Tum ek simple teacher ho jo mushkil cheezein aasaan bana sake. Mujhe samajhna hai ki AI kya hota hai aur kaise kaam karta hai — main bilkul beginner hoon, tech zyada nahi jaanta. Bilkul simple Hindi mein samjhao, daily life ke examples ke saath. English tech words avoid karo.
💪 Health
Tum ek certified fitness trainer ho jo ghar pe exercise jaanta ho. Mujhe ek weekly exercise routine chahiye — main 40 saal ki hoon, office mein 8 ghante baithti hoon, thoda weight lose karna chahti hoon. Har din 20-30 minute ka plan do, Hindi mein. Koi equipment nahi chahiye, crash diet suggest mat karo.
